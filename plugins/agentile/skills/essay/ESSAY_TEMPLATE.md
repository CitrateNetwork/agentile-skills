ESSAY TEMPLATE — Agentile.

Filename convention (Rule 12 + chronology):
  .agentile/docs/essays/YYYY-MM-DDTHHMM_<slug>.md

⚠️  ESSAYS ARE NOT GOVERNANCE.

Essays are conceptual arguments. They explore ideas, propose
frames, and connect work to broader patterns. They are HISTORICAL
CONTEXT, not operating rules. If a future session needs to know
"is this still how we do things?", the answer lives in
CORE_RULES.md, CONFIG.md, PRODUCT_SPEC.md, or the active SPRINT.md
— not in essays.

Essays survive across branches and eras even when the architecture
they describe has been replaced. That is a feature: they preserve
the reasoning behind decisions. It is also why they cannot bind
current work.

Write essays when an idea is large enough to outlive its sprint —
the minute when a journal would be too small and a case study
would be too anchored to a single incident.
-->
